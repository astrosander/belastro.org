Welcome to BelAstro

Dear ladies and gentlemen, students of schools, lyceums and gymnasiums, teachers and students, as well as everyone who is interested in astronomy and who practices it professionally!

A long-awaited event has taken place in the world of Belarusian astronomy - the official website of the Belarusian astronomical olympiads has started working. Only here will you find detailed official information related to olympiads at all levels, and in addition, many other useful materials and links.

In the "Olympiads" section - you can completely free download the conditions and solutions of tasks from previous years, the "Information" indicator will take you to the section with a file archive and links to astronomical resources on the Internet, in the "Photo Gallery" - a detailed photo report on past events of astronomical olympiads, you can communicate with us using the "Guest Book" and "Contacts" sections.
